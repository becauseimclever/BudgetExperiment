### Data Loading Strategy

1. `GetMonthlyCategoryReportAsync(year, month)` вҖ” **first await** (loading-state pin); builds treemap + waterfall data.
2. `GetTransactionsAsync(monthStart, monthEnd)` вҖ” feeds `BuildSpendingHeatmap`.
3. `GetBudgetSummaryAsync(year, month)` вҖ” derives `RadialBarSegment[]` and `RadarDataSeries[]`.

Each call wrapped in independent try-catch. Failure = empty data for that chart; page never crashes.
