**`@namespace` Required for Subdirectory Components:**
Razor files in `ApexCharts/` subdir default to namespace `...Charts.ApexCharts`. Code-behind declares `...Charts`. Mismatch → CS0115. Fix: `@namespace BudgetExperiment.Client.Components.Charts` in both `.razor` files.

**`IServiceProvider` for Optional Service Resolution:**
`[Inject] private IChartThemeService?` still throws `InvalidOperationException` in .NET 10 bUnit even as nullable. Fix: inject `IServiceProvider` (always available) and use `Services.GetService<T>()` for optional services. Pattern:
```csharp
var themeMode = Services.GetService<IChartThemeService>()?.GetApexChartsThemeMode() ?? "light";
```
